March 22, 2025 Cheese Team ANNOUNCEMENT

Cheese Client Comes to China:
Expanding Our Global Community

We're excited to announce that Cheese Client is now officially available to the massive Minecraft community in China! After months of intensive localization work and server optimization, Chinese players can now enjoy the full Cheese Client experience with native language support, local server compatibility, and dedicated community forums.

🇨🇳 Full Localization & Cultural Adaptation

Every part of Cheese Client has been carefully translated and adapted for Chinese players. The settings panel, HUD elements, and even tooltips are now available in Simplified Chinese, with plans for Traditional Chinese in a future update. We've also adjusted default keybinds to align with common preferences in the region and integrated with local Minecraft launchers to ensure a seamless setup process.

In‑game Chinese Wiki & Tutorials

To help new users get started, we've launched a Chinese knowledge base directly accessible from the client. It covers everything from installation to advanced module tuning, all written by native speakers familiar with both Cheese Client and the local Minecraft scene. Video tutorials produced in partnership with Chinese creators are also linked within the client.

⚡ Server Optimization for China

Chinese players often face higher latency when connecting to international servers. We've developed a smart routing system that detects the player's location and automatically selects the fastest connection path to Cheese Client's update servers and authentication services. Moreover, we've added pre‑configured profiles for major Chinese Minecraft networks (such as Hypercraft and Rainforest) that adjust timings and anti‑cheat evasion techniques to match their specific server environments.

✨ Early access feedback: During our beta test with 500 Chinese players, 94% reported a “significantly smoother” experience compared to using the international version. One tester noted: “终于有客户端真正考虑了中国玩家的网络环境，回弹和掉线几乎消失了。” (Finally a client that truly considers Chinese players' network conditions — stuttering and disconnects are almost gone.)

🌐 Dedicated Chinese Community Hubs

We believe great software needs great community support. Today we're opening official Cheese Client spaces on Discord China with native‑speaking moderators. These hubs will host regular Q&A sessions, event giveaways, and direct lines to our development team. We've also appointed three community managers from within the Chinese Minecraft community to ensure your voice shapes future updates.

🚀 What's Next: China‑Exclusive Features

This launch is just the beginning. Later this year we plan to introduce a “China Edition” module that integrates with the official Minecraft China client (by NetEase) while staying compliant with both Mojang guidelines and local regulations. We're also exploring partnerships with Chinese server networks to offer exclusive in‑client cosmetics inspired by Chinese mythology — all while keeping our core philosophy of performance and undetectability.

The Cheese Client 2.3.5 update, which includes all China localization features, is available for download immediately from our download page. Current users can simply update through the launcher.
