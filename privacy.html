Important Notice: Cheese Client is designed with privacy in mind. We believe in minimal data collection and maximum transparency. Read this policy to understand how we handle your information.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Cheese Client ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our software application, related services, and content (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy describes:

  • The types of information we may collect or that you may provide
  • How we use, store, and protect that information
  • Your rights regarding your personal data
  • How to contact us with privacy-related questions

By using Cheese Client, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Cheese Client is designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect minimal information necessary for the software to function properly.

Configuration Data

Settings, keybinds, and preferences you configure within Cheese Client. Stored locally on your device.

Crash Reports (Optional)

Anonymous technical data when the software crashes. Only sent with your explicit consent.

Usage Statistics (Optional)

Anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve performance. Opt-in only.

Detailed Information Categories:

Data Type What We Collect Storage Location Required/Optional
Application Settings Your configured preferences, modules, and keybinds Local device storage Required
Crash Reports Anonymous technical data, error logs, stack traces Secure servers (with consent) Optional
Performance Data FPS, memory usage, load times (aggregated) Secure servers (with consent) Optional
Minecraft Data Version, mod list, game settings (for compatibility) Local device only Required

What We Do NOT Collect: We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, IP address (except in crash reports), Minecraft account details, or any information that can personally identify you. We do not track your in-game activities, server connections, or chat messages.

3. How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect for specific purposes to improve Cheese Client:

Primary Uses:

  1. Software Functionality: To save and load your preferences, settings, and configurations
  2. Crash Resolution: To identify and fix software bugs and stability issues (with your consent)
  3. Performance Improvement: To optimize Cheese Client for different hardware configurations
  4. Compatibility: To ensure Cheese Client works correctly with different Minecraft versions and mods
  5. Feature Development: To understand which features are most used and prioritize development

Legal Basis for Processing:

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For optional data collection (crash reports, usage statistics)
  • Contractual Necessity: For data required to provide the software functionality
  • Legitimate Interests: For improving software quality and user experience

4. Data Storage & Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information:

Storage Locations:

  • Local Storage: All configuration data is stored locally on your device in encrypted configuration files
  • Cloud Storage: Only anonymous crash reports (with consent) are stored on secure servers
  • Retention Period: Crash reports are automatically deleted after 90 days

Security Measures:

  • Encryption: Configuration files are encrypted to prevent unauthorized access
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls on our servers
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are regularly reviewed and updated
  • Data Minimization: We only collect what is absolutely necessary

Security Notice: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

When We May Share Information:

  1. Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist us in operating our software (e.g., hosting providers for crash reports)
  2. Legal Requirements: When required by law or to respond to legal process
  3. Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Cheese Client, our users, or the public
  4. Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Third-Party Services Cheese Client May Access:

  • Minecraft Authentication: To verify game ownership (handled by Mojang/Microsoft)
  • Update Servers: To check for and download software updates
  • Crash Reporting Services: For anonymous crash reporting (with consent)

All third-party service providers are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of any data they process on our behalf.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Your Rights:

  • Access: You can access your local configuration data at any time
  • Correction: You can modify your settings directly within Cheese Client
  • Deletion: You can delete Cheese Client and all associated data from your device
  • Opt-Out: You can disable optional data collection in the settings menu
  • Data Portability: You can export your configuration to transfer to another device
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for optional data collection at any time

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  1. Open Cheese Client settings
  2. Navigate to the "Privacy" or "Data" section
  3. Adjust your preferences for data collection
  4. To delete all data, simply uninstall Cheese Client and delete the configuration folder

If you need assistance exercising any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 11.
